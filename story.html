Scene 1: A Happy Day for Yuki

On a sunny morning, Yuki put on her favorite blue clothes and happily walked out the door, ready to play with her friends.

Scene 2: Meeting the Little Rabbit

Yuki met a little rabbit on the grass. The rabbit looked a little lonely, so Yuki walked over and asked, "What's wrong, little rabbit?"

Scene 3: The Rabbit's Problem

The little rabbit said, "I lost my red apple, and I'm so hungry. I can't find it." Yuki decided to help the rabbit.

Scene 4: Looking Together

Yuki and the little rabbit searched everywhere for the red apple. They walked through the grass, crossed a small river, and climbed a little hill.

Scene 5: Finding the Red Apple

Finally, they found the red apple under a big tree! The little rabbit jumped up happily and thanked Yuki for her help.

Scene 6: Sharing the Apple

Yuki smiled and said, "Let's share this apple together." The little rabbit nodded happily, and the two friends sat on the grass, enjoying the delicious apple.

Scene 7: More Friends Arrive

Just then, Yuki heard the sound of her friend, the little bird, chirping. The little bird flew over and said, "I'm hungry too! Do you have any food?"

Scene 8: Sharing with Others

Yuki and the little rabbit didn't hesitate. They happily shared the apple with the little bird. The little bird said, "Thank you! You are the best friends!"

Scene 9: The Power of Friendship

The three friends played, laughed, and had so much fun together. They realized that sharing and helping friends made their friendship stronger.

Scene 10: A Happy Ending

As the sun began to set, Yuki and her new friends said goodbye, feeling warm and happy inside. They promised to play and share again tomorrow.
